What services do we offer?

Cardiology
we treat many cardiovascular conditions, such as: high blood pressure, high cholesterol, heart attack and stroke, heart rhythm problems, congestive heart failure, e.t.c.
Dermatology
We treat conditions such as eczema, skin cancer, acne, and psoriasis. We also perform some in-office procedures, including mole or spot removal, and we also offer aesthetic procedures.
Gynecology
we provide care for female health conditions like female reproductive health, cancer prevention and diagnosis in the reproductive organs, pregnancy, labor, infertility, menopause.
Pediatrics
well-baby and well-child visits, school or sports physicals, childhood vaccines, disease prevention for children and babies, treating illnesses and injuries in children and babies
Ophthalmology
we specialize in eye and vision care, treat diseases and conditions of the eyes and can perform eye surgery. We also see people for routine eye care, including prescribing glasses and performing checkups
General Surgery
we have training in diseases of the: abdomen, breast, endocrine system, gastrointestinal system, skin, and also care for a person both before and after their surgery.

What is the management system?

The management system is a simple way for you to get diagnosed and make appointments on your phone. You can just describe how you're feeling and the system will show you the most likely illness you're suffering from. This also helps give the doctors a heads up when you make an appointment and reduces the time spent before treatment can begin. You can also find some prescriptions made by our experts so you don't neccessarily have to go to the hospital.
